Our Story

Jigga Jerk Joint was born from a family recipe passed down through three generations — a secret marinade of scotch bonnet peppers, allspice, fresh thyme, and love. We brought those Jamaican roots to the streets and never looked back.

Every bird is marinated for a full 24 hours, then grilled slow over real wood fire. No shortcuts. Just bold, authentic flavor on every plate.
